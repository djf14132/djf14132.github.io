American Bulldogs

American Bulldogs are very well nurished, muscular dogs who have defined jaw lines.
They have very short hair and it's usually smooth. They are normally white, but you can find them with patches of brown, red, or black.These dogs are very athletic and can even jump up to 3 feet in the air. American Bulldogs have a high emotional personality so they need a lot of attention. They tolerate children
very well because they understand their limits. American Bulldogs tend to live from 10-16 years. Some common health problems found are:

  • Disorders of the kidney and thyroid
  • ACL tears
  • Bone cancer

Chocolate Labrador

Chocolate Labrador Retrievers are family-friendly and gentle dogs. They are very common in Austrailia, Canada, New Zealand, United Kingdom, and United States. They are very active dogs who love the water no matter what the temperature is. Chocolate Labs are easy to train, playful, and naturally obedient so they will make great family pets. They also make great hunting, tracking, or service dogs. Because of the restrictions provided by the standard, Labs are bred using the following color classes (note that Yellow Labs don't mix with Chocolate Labs because of the standard):

  • Blacks
  • Chocolates
  • Yellows
  • Blacks

Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are another popular type of dog. They can be used for hunting, drug dogs, or service dogs. Golden Retrievers also make great family pets because their face looks like they're smiling all of the time and they are friendly and playful. These dogs are great with children because they rarely bark and they are really calm and easy going. They need to stay active in order to prevent weight gain and promote overall health.
